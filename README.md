Termy is made to feel light, calm, and pleasant to use every day. It aims to stay out of your way, look good on your desktop, and give you a terminal that feels modern without becoming noisy or overbuilt.

It is still unapologetically a terminal, of course. Fast startup, solid fundamentals, and a UI that tries to be clean instead of clever.

## Why Termy

- Fast and minimal, with a little personality.
- Built with <a href="https://gpui.rs">GPUI</a> and <a href="https://alacritty.org">alacritty_terminal</a>.
- Focused on a native-feeling desktop experience instead of a browser-shaped terminal.
- Made with taste, not with fifty toggles for every pixel.
